1 5-Year Moving Average Detrended Share: The employment share is measured relative to its trailing 60-month (5-year) moving average. The reported value is the percent deviation of the current share from its recent 5-year baseline.
2 Hamilton Filter Log Share Detrended: The log of the employment share is regressed on its own values from 24–27 months earlier, following Hamilton (2018). The residual from this regression is taken as the detrended log share.
3 3rd-Degree Polynomial Log Employment Detrended: A third-degree polynomial time trend is fitted separately to the log of numerator and denominator employment. The detrended log share is constructed as the difference between the two residuals.
